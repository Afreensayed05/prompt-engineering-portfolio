# Persona Prompting — Cover Letter
---
## Bad Prompt
Write a cover letter for a tech job

##  AI Output (Bad)
Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to apply for a tech job at your company. I am interested in technology and enjoy learning new things.
I believe my skills and enthusiasm make me a good candidate for the position.
Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to hearing from you.

Sincerely,
Candidate

---

##  Good Prompt
You are a hiring manager at Google with 10 years 
of experience selecting top AI talent.

Write a cover letter for a fresher applying for 
an AI Engineer internship role.

About the candidate:
- Computer Science student from India
- Earned Google Cloud Prompt Engineering Badge
- Knows prompt engineering techniques

Tone: Confident but not arrogant
Length: Max 200 words
Format: 3 short paragraphs

## AI Output (Good)
Dear Hiring Manager,

I am a Computer Science student from India with a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence and emerging technologies. Through continuous learning and hands-on practice, I have developed foundational skills in prompt engineering and earned the Google Cloud Prompt Engineering Badge, strengthening my understanding of AI systems and effective model interaction.

I enjoy solving problems, experimenting with AI tools, and applying structured prompting techniques to improve the quality and relevance of AI-generated outputs. These experiences have enhanced my analytical thinking, adaptability, and ability to learn new technologies quickly.

I am excited about the opportunity to contribute as an AI Engineer Intern, gain industry experience, and continue growing alongside experienced professionals. Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to the opportunity to discuss how I can contribute to your team.

Sincerely,
Sayed Afreen

---

## What Changed
- Added persona → hiring manager at Google
- Added candidate details → real context
- Specified tone → confident but not arrogant
- Added format → 3 paragraphs, 200 words max
- Output felt personal, professional and targeted
